SIOP Forecast Performance Dashboard 
Business Objective : 
Developed an interactive SIOP dashboard to analyze Revenue, Non
Revenue and CAPEX forecasts against actuals. Implemented lag-based 
forecast comparison, forecast accuracy, forecast bias and country-level 
performance analysis. 
Data preparation: 
• Imported Forecast and Actual datasets 
• Created lag mapping (Resultant, Lag0, Lag1) 
• Pivoted forecast measures 
• Built fact and dimension tables 
• Created relationships between dimensions and fact table 
KPI’s Developed : 
• Revenue Forecast 
• Non Revenue Forecast 
• CAPEX Forecast 
• Forecast Accuracy % 
• Forecast Bias % 
• Forecast Error 
Key Insights: 
• Forecast Accuracy: 66% 
• Forecast Bias: -34% 
• Country A contributes highest forecast revenue 
• Forecast underestimates actual demand 
Lag Selection Example 
Lag0 represents the latest forecast snapshot available before the actual 
sales period. It is used to compare planned demand against actual business 
performance. Analyzing Lag0 helps measure forecast accuracy and 
identify forecasting gaps. This enables better demand planning and 
decision-making in the SIOP process. 
Country Filter Example 
Country A is the highest contributor across Revenue, Non-Revenue, and 
CAPEX forecasts. The dashboard indicates that the majority of forecasted 
business activity is concentrated in Country A compared to other 
countries. However, the Forecast Accuracy of 52.73% and Forecast Bias 
of -47.27% suggest that actual demand is significantly higher than 
forecasted values, indicating an under-forecasting trend. This highlights 
the need for improved demand planning and forecast refinement for 
Country A to better align forecasts with actual business performance.
